Codex Status Light Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

What We Collect

Codex Status Light does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data. The app includes no ads, analytics SDKs, user accounts, or cloud services.

Local Access Only

The app only reads local session state files inside your .codex folder after you explicitly grant access. It uses that data to display run, completion, or disconnect status, and nothing leaves your Mac.

Permission Management

You can revoke access at any time by removing the app or by revoking the folder permission in System Settings.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email huoyuan461@qq.com.
